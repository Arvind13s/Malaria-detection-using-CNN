About

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by Plasmodium parasites transmitted through infected mosquito bites. Timely and accurate microscopic diagnosis is critical — manual examination of blood smears is labour-intensive and error-prone.

MalariaVision uses a Convolutional Neural Network trained on the NIH Malaria Cell Images dataset to automate detection. The model classifies individual cell images as either containing Plasmodium parasites (Parasitized) or being free of infection (Uninfected).

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool is intended for educational and research purposes only. It is not a certified medical device and should not be used for clinical diagnosis.
